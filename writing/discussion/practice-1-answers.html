📋 ディスカッション内容の確認📋 Discussion recap
Dr. Nakamura
Should cities prioritize preserving urban green spaces, or should they allow more construction to address housing needs? Why?
Priya（緑地保全派）Priya (pro–green spaces)
公園は精神的健康に良い。自然へのアクセスが不安やうつを減らすという研究がある。Parks are good for mental health. Studies show that access to nature reduces anxiety and depression.
Marcus（住宅建設派）Marcus (pro–housing construction)
家賃が高すぎる。手頃な住宅を増やすべき。緑地はルーフトップガーデンなどで代替可能。Rent is too high. We need more affordable housing. Green spaces can be replaced by rooftop gardens.
✅ 模範回答（約115語）✅ Sample response (~115 words)
主張Claim I believe that cities should try to balance both preserving green spaces and building new housing, rather than choosing one over the other.

言及Reference While I understand Marcus's concern about high rent, I agree with Priya that green spaces are essential for residents' mental health.

理由Reason The key is finding creative solutions that address both needs. For example, cities can require developers to include green areas within new housing projects, such as courtyards, rooftop gardens, or ground-level parks.

具体例Example In my hometown, a new apartment complex was built with a large community garden on the first floor. Residents get affordable housing and access to green space at the same time.

So overall, I think smart urban planning can meet housing demands without sacrificing the natural spaces that make cities livable.

※ この模範回答は「第3の立場」（バランス型）を取っています。どちらか一方に賛成する形でもOKです。Note: this sample takes a "third position" (balanced view). Agreeing with one side is also fine.

✅ 自己チェックリスト✅ Self-check list
☑️ 最初の1文で立場を明確にしている（I believe that...）First sentence states the position clearly (I believe that...)
☑️ 他の学生の意見に言及している（I agree with... / While I understand...）References another student's view (I agree with... / While I understand...)
☑️ 明確な理由が1つ以上あるAt least one clear reason
☑️ 具体例が1つある（自分の経験でOK）One concrete example (personal experience is fine)
☑️ 100語以上あるAt least 100 words
☑️ まとめの文で締めくくっている（So overall...）Closes with a wrap-up sentence (So overall...)
⚠️ よくあるミス⚠️ Common mistakes
✗ 立場が不明確✗ Unclear position

"This is a very interesting question..." で始めて立場を述べない → I believe that... ですぐ始めるDon't open with "This is a very interesting question..." without stating a position. Start directly with "I believe that..."

✗ 理由が2つで浅い✗ Two shallow reasons

2つの理由を浅く並べるより、1つの理由を深く掘り下げる方が高得点One deep reason scores higher than two shallow ones

✗ 他の学生への言及なし✗ No reference to other students

ディスカッションなので他の投稿に触れることが期待されているIt's a discussion — referencing other posts is expected

✗ 具体例が長すぎる✗ Example too long

具体例は2-3文で十分。長すぎると時間切れのリスク2–3 sentences is enough. Too long risks running out of time
