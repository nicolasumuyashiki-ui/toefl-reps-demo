💡 Build a Sentence 攻略 3ステップ💡 Build a Sentence — 3-step strategy

1

主語と動詞を見つけるFind the subject and verb

「誰が（主語）」「どうする（動詞）」を最初に探す。この2つが文の骨格。Look for "who (subject)" and "does what (verb)" first. These two are the skeleton of the sentence.

2

骨格を作るBuild the skeleton

S+V を組み合わせて基本形を作る。文型（SV, SVC, SVO）を意識。Combine S+V into a basic form. Be aware of sentence patterns (SV, SVC, SVO).

3

残りを配置Place the rest

修飾語、前置詞句、接続詞を適切な位置に。最後に音読チェック（5秒）。Position modifiers, prepositional phrases, and conjunctions correctly. Finish with a 5-second read-aloud check.
