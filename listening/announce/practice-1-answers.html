💡 Announcement 攻略のコツ

🎯

4つの焦点で聞く

WHO（誰が）・WHAT（何を）・WHEN（いつ）・WHERE（どこで）を聞き逃さない

📍

目的問題は冒頭に答え

「What is the announcement about?」の答えは最初の1〜2文にある。冒頭に全集中！

🔚

推奨・次のアクションは末尾

「What does the speaker recommend?」の答えは最後の文に隠れていることが多い

📢 Announcement 1 — Field Trip の行き先変更

場面：教室 ｜ 話者：男性教授

📜 スクリプトを見る（クリックで展開）
[NARRATOR] Listen to an announcement in a classroom.
[SPEAKER] Alright everyone, before we wrap up today, I have a quick update about the field trip next Friday. We won't be going to the natural history museum as originally planned. Instead, we'll visit the botanical garden on the east side of campus. Please meet at the main entrance by nine a.m., and make sure to bring a notebook for the observation activity. The bus will leave at exactly nine fifteen, so don't be late.
1
What change does the professor announce?
Detail
(A) A different destination for the field trip.
(B) A new date for the field trip.
(C) A cancellation of the observation activity.
(D) A change in the class meeting time.
解説： 教授は「We won't be going to the natural history museum... Instead, we'll visit the botanical garden」と行き先の変更を明言。日付（next Friday）は変わっていないので(B)は不正解。
🔑 WHAT に注目：「何が変わったか」を聞いている。museum → botanical garden = different destination。
2
What does the professor ask students to bring?
Detail
(A) A camera for the garden tour.
(B) A notebook for the observation activity.
(C) A textbook for the lecture.
(D) A ticket for the bus ride.
解説： 「make sure to bring a notebook for the observation activity」と明確に述べている。garden/bus などアナウンスに出てきた単語を含む(A)(D)は引っかけ。
🔑 関連語の罠に注意：「garden」が出てくるので(A)のcameraを選びたくなるが、本文では notebook としか言っていない。

📢 Announcement 2 — 無料インフルエンザ予防接種

場面：キャンパスラジオ ｜ 話者：女性アナウンサー

📜 スクリプトを見る（クリックで展開）
[NARRATOR] Listen to an announcement on the campus radio station.
[SPEAKER] Hey everyone, this is a reminder that the student health center will be offering free flu shots starting this Monday through Friday from ten a.m. to four p.m. No appointment is needed — just bring your student ID. The health center is on the second floor of the Whitfield Building. Last year, spots filled up fast in the afternoon, so we recommend coming in the morning if you can.
3
What is the announcement mainly about?
Main Idea
(A) A change in the health center's location.
(B) An update on campus safety procedures.
(C) Free flu vaccinations for students.
(D) A new requirement for student health insurance.
解説： 冒頭で「the student health center will be offering free flu shots」と目的を明示。Main Idea問題は最初の1〜2文に答えがある。(A)はlocationに言及しているが「変更」ではない。
🔑 目的問題の鉄則：冒頭に全集中。「offering free flu shots」= Free flu vaccinations for students。
4
Why does the announcer suggest coming in the morning?
Inference
(A) The health center closes early in the afternoon.
(B) Morning appointments are less expensive.
(C) The parking lot gets full in the afternoon.
(D) Afternoon spots filled up quickly last year.
解説： 「Last year, spots filled up fast in the afternoon, so we recommend coming in the morning」— 昨年の経験に基づく推奨。「spots filled up fast」= filled up quickly のパラフレーズ。
🔑 推奨の根拠は末尾に：「so we recommend...」の直前にある理由が答え。(A)は「closes early」だが本文では4pmまで開いていると明言。
