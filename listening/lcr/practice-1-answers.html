💡 LCR 攻略のコツStrategy tips
1
最初の2語に全集中 — Do you→Yes/No、What→Wh疑問文、Could you→依頼、How about→提案Lock in on the first two words — Do you → Yes/No, What → Wh-question, Could you → request, How about → suggestion
2
質問タイプを判断 — Yes/No、5W1H、依頼、提案、確認など。タイプに合う応答パターンを予測Identify the question type — Yes/No, 5W1H, request, suggestion, confirmation, etc. Predict a response pattern that matches the type
3
自然な応答を選ぶ — 「直接的すぎる答え」は疑う。会話を前に進める選択肢＝正解Pick the natural response — be suspicious of "too-direct" answers. The option that moves the conversation forward = correct
4
否定形に注意 — Isn't...? / Don't you...? は確認の意味。意味を逆に取らないWatch for negative forms — Isn't...? / Don't you...? are confirmation questions. Don't reverse the meaning
