💡 Conversation 攻略のコツStrategy tips

📝

メモの取り方

問題は何か？解決策は何か？をキーワードだけメモ。全文書き取ろうとしない！

🔄

問題解決パターン

挨拶→問題提示→解決策提案→結論。キャンパス会話の多くはこのパターンに従う

😮

感情表現に注目

Oh, that's a relief! / That's frustrating. / Really? → 話者の感情は頻出の設問ポイント

🗣️ Conversation 1 — 寮の部屋変更

場面：学生（女性）が住居課スタッフ（男性）に部屋の変更を相談

📜 スクリプトを見る（クリックで展開）
[NARRATOR] Listen to a conversation.
[WOMAN] Hi, is it possible to switch dorm rooms this semester? Mine's on the ground floor, and the parking lot noise makes it really hard to study.
[MAN] I understand. We do have a few openings on the upper floors. You'd need to fill out a transfer request form, though.
[WOMAN] Sure. How long does the process take?
[MAN] About two weeks. I can give you the form right now, if you'd like.
[WOMAN] That'd be great. Thanks.
[MAN] Here you go. Just bring it back signed, and we'll get things started.
1
Why does the woman want to change her room?
Detail
(A) There is too much outside noise near her room.
(B) Her roommate is too noisy.
(C) Her room is too far from her classes.
(D) The heating system in her room is broken.
解説： 女性は「the parking lot noise makes it really hard to study」と明言。駐車場の騒音＝outside noiseが正解根拠。
🔑 ヒント位置：会話の冒頭（女性の1発話目）— 問題提示パターンの典型。最初の発話で理由が語られる。
2
What does the man give the woman?
Detail
(A) A list of available rooms.
(B) A form she needs to complete.
(C) A key to a new room.
(D) A map of the east wing.
解説： 男性は「I can give you the form right now」「Here you go. Just bring it back signed」と言っている。transfer request form = a form she needs to complete。
🔑 ヒント位置：会話の終盤 — 解決策→次のアクションの部分。「何を渡したか」は会話の締めに出やすい。

🗣️ Conversation 2 — ボランティア清掃イベント

場面：2人の学生が土曜日の公園清掃イベントの準備について相談

📜 スクリプトを見る（クリックで展開）
[NARRATOR] Listen to a conversation.
[MAN] Hey, did you hear back from the community center about Saturday's cleanup?
[WOMAN] Yeah, they confirmed the park, but we need to bring our own trash bags. Their budget got cut.
[MAN] Oh, I thought they were providing those. How many people signed up?
[WOMAN] About twenty. I was thinking each person could bring a box of bags.
[MAN] Good idea. I'll send out a group message tonight. What time are we meeting?
[WOMAN] Nine a.m., at the main entrance of Riverside Park.
[MAN] Got it. I'll include that in the message.
3
Why do the students need to bring their own supplies?
Inference
(A) The event was planned at the last minute.
(B) The park does not allow outside equipment.
(C) The volunteers forgot to request supplies.
(D) The community center has less funding this semester.
解説： 女性は「Their budget got cut」と言っている。budget got cut = less funding。直接「funding」とは言っていないため推論問題（パラフレーズの発見）。
🔑 推論のコツ：「budget got cut」→ 予算削減 → less funding。本文の言い換えが正解。本文と同じ単語を使う選択肢は疑え。
4
What will the man do next?
Detail
(A) Contact the community center for more details.
(B) Buy trash bags and gloves for the group.
(C) Send a message to the volunteer group.
(D) Ask each volunteer to sign up again.
解説： 男性は「I'll send out a group message tonight」「I'll include that in the message」と2回言及。send out a group message = send a message to the volunteer group。
🔑 ヒント位置：会話の最後 — 「次に何をするか」は常に会話の締めに出る。最後の1〜2文に全集中！
