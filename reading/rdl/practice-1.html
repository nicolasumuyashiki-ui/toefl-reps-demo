Subject: New Quiet Floor Policy — Effective October 1
From: Sarah Chen, Head Librarian <s.chen@riverside.edu> To: All Library Users Date: September 22, 2025

Dear students and faculty,

Starting October 1, the third and fourth floors of the Main Library will be designated silent study zones. No talking, phone calls, or group work will be permitted on these floors. Group discussion and collaborative study should take place on the first floor in the Café Annex, which now offers extended hours until 11 PM Sunday through Thursday.

To help maintain the new policy, we will install noise-level sensors on the silent floors beginning November 1. These sensors will trigger a soft chime if voices rise above a quiet conversational level. Repeated alerts may result in a temporary loss of library access.

If you require group study space outside the Café Annex's hours, the Student Union meeting rooms remain available by reservation through the library website.

Best regards,
Sarah Chen
Head Librarian, Riverside University
